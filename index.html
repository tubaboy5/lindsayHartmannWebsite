About Me

My work career hasn't been a straight path, but it has lead me to right where I want to be. After working extensively in retail loss prevention, I realized that helping people navigate their career journey is where I'm happiest. From Walmart to Target and Amazon to Infosys, I'm constantly striving to make employees time at work the best it can be. Outside of my work, I live just south of St. Louis, MO in a suburb named Arnold with my husband and pets. We have 2 dogs and 2 cats.
